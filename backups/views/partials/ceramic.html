Why BAMBOO

Bamboo flooring is an extremely stable hardwood, a true reliable and consistent product. 99.5% resistant to termites. And it is healthy, with No toxic fumes. Reasonably prices comparable to hardwood timbers.

COATING

All our solid Bamboo flooring is protected against insects, rot and mildew during the manufacturing process. The surfaces are pre-finished with 8 layers German manufactured water-based Polyurethane. The finishes are standard Semi-Gloss or Matt.
